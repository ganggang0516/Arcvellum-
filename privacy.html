Arcvellum 隐私政策
Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-06-22

我们处理什么 / What We Process

Arcvellum 保存你主动提交的网页链接、网页正文、图片、标签、阅读状态和文件夹信息。应用不要求注册账号，不接入广告，也不使用跨 App 追踪。

Arcvellum stores links, page content, images, tags, reading status, and folders that you choose to save. The app does not require an account, show ads, or track you across apps.

存储与同步 / Storage and Sync

内容保存在你的设备中。启用 iCloud 时，内容通过 Apple CloudKit 存入与你 Apple ID 关联的私有数据库。开发者不会通过自有服务器读取或出售这些内容。

Content is stored on your device. When iCloud is enabled, it is stored in the private CloudKit database associated with your Apple ID. The developer does not read or sell this content through a developer-operated server.

第三方网站 / Third-Party Websites

为解析你保存的内容，应用会直接请求对应网站及其图片或媒体地址。相关网站可能按其隐私政策接收 IP 地址、User-Agent、Cookie 或必要的网络请求信息。

To parse saved content, the app connects directly to the relevant website and its image or media hosts. Those services may receive your IP address, user agent, cookies, or other request data under their own privacy policies.

剪切板 / Clipboard

应用只会在你主动点击“粘贴”或“复制原网址”时读取或写入剪切板，不会在后台持续读取剪切板。

The app reads or writes the clipboard only when you tap Paste or Copy Original URL. It does not continuously read the clipboard in the background.

购买 / Purchases

订阅由 Apple 处理。开发者不会接收你的银行卡信息；应用只读取订阅是否有效以及产品标识。

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. The developer does not receive your payment-card details; the app only reads entitlement status and product identifiers.

算法与个性化 / Algorithms and Personalization

应用使用规则型网页解析、去噪和本地分类帮助整理内容，不建立广告画像，不进行跨 App 追踪，也不根据个人资料做影响权益的自动化决策。

The app uses rule-based page parsing, cleanup, and local classification to organize content. It does not build advertising profiles, track you across apps, or make automated decisions that affect your rights.

控制与删除 / Control and Deletion

你可以随时删除内容。删除项目进入“最近删除”并在 30 天后自动永久删除，也可以立即清空。

You can delete content at any time. Deleted items remain in Recently Deleted for 30 days unless permanently removed sooner.
