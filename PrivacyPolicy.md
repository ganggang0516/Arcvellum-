# Arcvellum Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Effective date / 生效日期: June 14, 2026 / 2026年6月14日

## English

Arcvellum stores links, page content, images, media, tags, reading status, and
collections that you choose to save. The app does not require a developer
account, display advertising, sell personal information, or track you across
apps and websites.

Content is stored on your device. When iCloud is available, it may be synced
through Apple's CloudKit to the private database associated with your Apple ID.
The developer does not operate a server that reads or sells your saved library.

To parse content that you request, the app connects directly to the relevant
website and its image or media hosts. Those third parties may receive ordinary
network information such as your IP address, user agent, cookies, and requested
URL under their own privacy policies.

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. The developer does not receive your
payment-card information. The app reads only the product identifier and
subscription entitlement status needed to provide Pro features.

Arcvellum uses rule-based page parsing, cleanup, and local classification to
organize content you choose to save. It does not build advertising profiles,
track you across apps, or use automated decisions that affect pricing,
subscription eligibility, credit, employment, education, medical, or other
significant matters.

If you use AI Insight, supported models run locally on your device. The article
text used for summary and interpretation is not sent to a cloud AI service by
Arcvellum. Model files that you import are stored in the app sandbox.

The pasteboard is accessed only when you tap Paste. The app does not monitor or
read the pasteboard automatically.

Deleted items remain in Recently Deleted for 30 days unless you permanently
delete them sooner. You may also disable iCloud or remove the app's iCloud data
through Apple system settings.

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information on the Arcvellum
App Store product page.

## 中文

Arcvellum 保存你主动提交的网页链接、正文、图片、媒体、标签、阅读状态和收藏夹信息。应用不要求注册开发者账号，不展示广告，不出售个人信息，也不进行跨应用或跨网站追踪。

内容保存在你的设备中。iCloud 可用时，内容可能通过 Apple CloudKit 同步至与你 Apple ID 关联的私有数据库。开发者不运营读取或出售用户资料库的服务器。

为解析你主动保存的内容，应用会直接请求对应网站及其图片或媒体地址。相关第三方可能依据其隐私政策接收 IP 地址、User-Agent、Cookie 和所请求的网址等常规网络信息。

订阅由 Apple 处理。开发者不会收到你的银行卡信息。应用仅读取提供 Pro 功能所需的产品标识和订阅权益状态。

Arcvellum 使用规则型网页解析、清理和本地分类来整理你主动保存的内容。应用不建立广告画像，不进行跨应用追踪，也不会使用自动化决策影响价格、订阅资格、信用、就业、教育、医疗或其他重大事项。

如果你使用 AI 解读，受支持的模型会在设备本地运行。Arcvellum 不会把用于摘要和解读的文章正文发送到云端 AI 服务。你导入的模型文件保存在应用沙盒中。

应用仅在你点击“粘贴”时访问剪贴板，不会自动监控或读取剪贴板。

删除内容会在“最近删除”中保留30天，除非你提前永久删除。你也可以通过 Apple 系统设置关闭 iCloud 或删除应用的 iCloud 数据。

如有隐私问题，请使用 Arcvellum App Store 产品页中提供的开发者联系方式联系我们。
